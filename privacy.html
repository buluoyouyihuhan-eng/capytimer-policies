🐾 Privacy Policy

Capy Timer  ·  Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Summary: Capy Timer is an app intended for users aged 13 and older. Most app settings are stored locally on your device. The Android app may use store services for purchases and, for Russian-language users, rewarded advertising to unlock temporary Pro access.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

Capy Timer does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Hardware identifiers such as IMEI or MAC address
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or approximate)
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • Browsing history or app usage data
  • Cookies or cross-app tracking data
  • Health or biometric data

1.1a Health Data Declaration (Google Play Policy)

As Capy Timer is categorized under "Health & Fitness" for relaxation and focus purposes, we explicitly declare that:

  • No Health Data: We do NOT collect, store, access, or share any personal health data, physiological measurements, or sensitive medical information.
  • Not Medical Advice: Capy Timer is a general wellness tool and is not intended for medical diagnosis or treatment.

1.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following preferences are saved only on your device using Android SharedPreferences (Android) or iOS UserDefaults (iOS):

  • Timer duration preference (e.g., 30 minutes)
  • Selected sleep sound
  • Selected "Then" action (app to launch or sound to play)
  • Pro purchase status or temporary rewarded-access expiry
  • App launch count (for onboarding display logic)

These app preferences are used to provide the app experience and remember your settings between sessions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use locally stored preferences to deliver the app experience, remember your settings, and manage Pro access status. Where rewarded ads are available, the advertising provider may process data according to its own privacy policy.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local-Only Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Android: SharedPreferences, protected by Android OS-level sandboxing
  • iOS: UserDefaults, protected by iOS app sandbox

We operate no servers, no databases, and no cloud infrastructure for user data storage.

3.2 Audio and Animation Assets

All audio files (sleep sounds) and animations (capybara graphics) are bundled directly within the app package. No network request is made to stream or download content after installation.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Payment Processing

In-app purchases (Pro version) are processed entirely by the platform's store:

  • Google Play Store (Android): Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store (iOS): Apple's Privacy Policy

We do not receive, store, or process any payment card or billing information. All transactions are handled exclusively by Google and Apple.

4.2 Rewarded Advertising

On Android, Russian-language users may be offered rewarded ads to unlock temporary Pro access. Rewarded ads are provided through Yandex Mobile Ads SDK / Yandex Advertising Network.

Yandex may process device, advertising, and ad interaction data as described in its own privacy policy and partner terms:

  • Yandex Privacy Policy
  • Yandex Partner Terms

4.3 Other Third-Party Services

Except for platform store services and the rewarded advertising described above, Capy Timer does not use or integrate:

  • Analytics platforms (Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Social login or social SDKs

5. Permissions

Capy Timer requests the following system permissions:

5.1 Android Permissions

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW (Draw over other apps): Required to display the floating capybara overlay during focus sessions. No data is collected through this permission.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK: Required to keep the overlay and sleep sound active when the app is in the background.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to show a status notification while the focus timer is running. No notification content is transmitted off-device.
  • VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback at milestone moments. No data collected.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Not currently used (reserved for future auto-start feature).

5.2 iOS Permissions

  • Live Activities (Dynamic Island / Lock Screen): Used to display the capybara timer on the Dynamic Island and lock screen. No data leaves the device.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share locally stored app preferences with third parties. Where rewarded ads are available, ad requests and ad measurement are handled by the advertising provider under its own policies. We will never sell your information.

We may disclose information only if required by law (e.g., valid legal process), but as we hold no user data, this is practically impossible.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Deletion

To delete all app data, simply uninstall Capy Timer from your device. All locally stored preferences will be permanently removed. No data exists anywhere else to delete.

7.2 Access to Your Data

You can review all stored preferences at any time via your device's app settings. On Android: Settings → Apps → Capy Timer → Clear data. On iOS: uninstalling the app removes all data.

7.3 No Opt-Out Needed

Because we do not collect any personal data or use tracking, there is nothing to opt out of.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA & Equivalent)

Capy Timer is not directed at children under 13. The app is a productivity and sleep-aid tool intended for users aged 13 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 is using the app, please contact us at capytimer.help@outlook.com and we will take appropriate action.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold. Because Capy Timer collects no personal information, we do not sell any personal data and the rights to access, delete, and opt-out of sale are automatically satisfied.

To submit a verifiable consumer request, contact: capytimer.help@outlook.com.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

Capy Timer does not process personal data of EU/EEA residents. As no personal data is collected, there is no legal basis required for processing, and no Data Protection Officer is required.

EU residents retain all rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. As we hold no data, these rights are inherently fulfilled.

We do not transfer personal data to third countries because we do not transfer personal data at all.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app's features change. Significant changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: capytimer.help@outlook.com
  • App Name: Capy Timer
  • Response time: We aim to respond within 5 business days.
