Welcome to Capy Timer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Free Users

If you use the free version of Capy Timer, we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app functionality operates entirely on your device.

1.2 Pro Users (Lifetime Purchase)

When you purchase the Pro version ($2.99 one-time payment), we store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Sound Preferences: Your last selected sleep sound
  • Timer Settings: Your preferred sleep timer duration
  • "Then" Action: Your selected post-sleep action (app to launch or sound to play)
  • Pro Purchase Status: A flag indicating you have purchased Pro features

Important: This data never leaves your device. It is stored using Android SharedPreferences or iOS UserDefaults and is not transmitted to any server.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect:

  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Advertising IDs
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

The locally stored preferences (Pro users only) are used solely to:

  • Remember your last settings when you reopen the app
  • Provide a personalized sleep experience
  • Restore your Pro status if you reinstall the app (via Apple/Google purchase verification)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Android: SharedPreferences (encrypted at the OS level)
  • iOS: UserDefaults (secured by iOS Keychain when applicable)

3.2 Firebase Storage

We use Firebase Storage (a Google Cloud service) to host and deliver premium audio files to Pro users. When you stream or download a Pro audio file:

  • Your device connects directly to Firebase Storage
  • No personal data is sent to Firebase (no user ID, email, or device info)
  • Firebase may log anonymous technical data (IP address, timestamp) as per Firebase's Privacy Policy

3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • All data is stored locally with OS-level encryption
  • Audio files are served over HTTPS
  • We do not maintain any user databases or servers

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Payment Processing

  • Apple App Store (iOS): Processes in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store (Android): Processes in-app purchases. See Google's Privacy Policy

We do not receive or store your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple/Google.

4.2 Firebase Storage

Used solely for audio file distribution. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

We may disclose information only if required by law (e.g., court order, subpoena) or to protect our legal rights.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible to you at any time
  • Delete: Use the "Delete My Data" option in Settings to clear all local preferences
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all local data permanently

6.2 Opt-Out

Since we don't collect analytics or tracking data, there is nothing to opt out of.

6.3 Data Portability

Your preferences are stored in a standard format on your device. You can export them using device backup tools (iCloud, Google Drive).

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Capy Timer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our app:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect personal data from any users
  • Does not contain third-party advertising
  • Does not enable communication with other users

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at capytimer.help@outlook.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Firebase Storage servers may be located in various regions, but no personal data is transmitted.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected (none, except local preferences for Pro users)
  • Request deletion of your data (use "Delete My Data" in Settings)
  • Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Rectify inaccurate data (edit in Settings)
  • Erase your data (use "Delete My Data")
  • Restrict processing (uninstall the app)
  • Data portability (export via device backup)
  • Object to processing (we don't process personal data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at capytimer.help@outlook.com.
